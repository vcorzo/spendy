Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when you create an account (name, email address, and password) and the expense records you enter while using the app (amounts, categories, dates, and optional descriptions). We do not collect payment information or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (such as cloud hosting) to operate the service; these providers are contractually bound to keep your data confidential and may not use it for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will respond within 30 days.
